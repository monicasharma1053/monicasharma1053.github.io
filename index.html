MasterCard Partnership (expected launch: June 2016)

I took this project from a brainstorm into a completed functioning product. I took an Agile/Lean UX approach and began by defining assumptions with business stakeholders and identifying the biggest potential for risk. I created and distributed surveys among potential customers and then conducted initial user conversations and research to identify behaviors and motivations. I worked side by side with the Development team(Software Engineers, UI Engineers, QA,and Database), Product Manager, and Strategy and Operations. I developed the sitemap, created personas based on our initial user research, utilized card sorting techniques for the Registration process, and information architecture (tree-jack) for Account Home. UX stayed an iteration ahead of the development team to test live prototypes throughout the lifecycle.

We worked in a Zurb Foundations Development framework and implemented HAML, CSS, Javascript, and Ruby. I utilized my front-end development skills to assist the development team and contribute to front-end implementation and styling.
